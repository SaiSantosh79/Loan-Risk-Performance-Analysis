# 📊 Loan Risk & Performance Analysis

## 🔍 Business Problem
Financial institutions need to monitor loan portfolio performance and minimize default risk.

This project answers:
**How can we identify high-performing vs risky loan segments, optimize lending strategy, and improve repayment performance?**

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze loan applications (overall & MTD)
- Evaluate funded vs received amounts (cash flow health)
- Identify Good vs Bad loans
- Assess borrower risk using DTI & interest rates
- Analyze trends across:
  - Time
  - Region
  - Loan purpose
  - Employment length
  - Home ownership

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 1. Portfolio Health
- ~86% Good Loans → Stable portfolio
- ~14% Bad Loans → Default risk exposure  
➡️ Needs improved risk filtering

---

### 2. Cash Flow Analysis
- Total received ≈ Total funded  
➡️ Strong repayment behavior and healthy cash flow

---

### 3. Loan Trends
- Consistent growth with seasonal spikes  
➡️ Useful for demand forecasting

---

### 4. Risk Analysis
- High interest rates linked to high-risk borrowers  
➡️ Reflects risk-based pricing

- High DTI → Higher default probability  
➡️ Strong predictor of loan risk

---

### 5. Borrower Behavior
- Longer employment → Lower risk  
- Homeowners → Better repayment patterns  
➡️ Financial stability reduces default risk

---

### 6. Regional Insights
- Variation in loan volume and risk across regions  
➡️ Enables targeted lending strategies

---

### 7. Loan Purpose
- Majority loans used for:
  - Debt consolidation
  - Personal use  
➡️ Indicates financial management needs

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Tighten approval for high DTI borrowers  
- Focus on:
  - Stable employment segments  
  - Homeowners  
- Monitor high-risk regions  
- Optimize interest rates vs default risk  
- Expand lending in high-performing segments  

---

This project demonstrates how data-driven insights can:
